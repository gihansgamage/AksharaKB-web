What's included

Everything you need
to type in three languages.

⌨️
Wijesekara Layout
The standard Sri Lankan Sinhala keyboard layout with all 58 consonants, vowels, and special characters including ZWJ compounds.
🔤
Phonetic Input
Type Sinhala and Tamil phonetically using English letters. ka → ක, la → ල. Smart greedy matching for natural typing flow.
🌐
Three Languages
Seamlessly switch between Sinhala, Tamil, and English with one tap. All three active simultaneously in the candidate bar.
✨
Liquid Glass UI
Frosted glass keys with real-time window blur, soft shadows, and top-shine gradients. Fully adapts to dark and light themes.
🔮
Word Predictions
Local word prediction with learning. English suggestions enhanced by Google Suggest API. Emoji suggestions for expressive typing.
😀
Emoji Keyboard
Full emoji keyboard with 1600+ emojis across 12 categories, recent history, scrollable grid, and quick action bar.
🖖
Long-Press Popups
Long-press any key to reveal alternate characters. ග → ඟ, ද → ධ/ඳ, ජ → ඦ. Custom popup window built entirely in-app.
📱
Smart Auto-caps
Automatic capitalization at sentence starts. Three-state caps: single shift, caps lock, and off. Works across all three languages.
🎛️
Symbols + More
Full symbols keyboard with shifted layer for ©®™€£¥. Clipboard history, haptic feedback, and popup key previews.

How to use

User Guide

⚙️ Setup

1
Open Settings → System → Language & Input → Manage Keyboards and enable AksharaKB.
2
Tap any text field, then tap the keyboard icon in the system bar and choose AksharaKB.
3
Open the app and tap Keyboard Settings to choose your theme, languages, and Sinhala layout.
4
Tap 🌐En in the candidate bar to cycle through your enabled languages.

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

AksharaKB ("the App") is a free, open-source Android keyboard application developed by Gihan S Gamage. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and what we do not collect.

We are committed to your privacy. AksharaKB is designed to work entirely on your device, and we do not collect, store, or transmit your personal data or keystrokes.

Information We Do NOT Collect

  • We do not collect or log any text you type.
  • We do not transmit your keystrokes, messages, passwords, or clipboard content to any server.
  • We do not collect personal identifiers such as name, email, phone number, or device ID.
  • We do not use analytics SDKs or crash reporting services that transmit personal data.
  • We do not display advertisements.

Internet Access

The App requests INTERNET permission solely to fetch English word suggestions from Google's autocomplete API (suggestqueries.google.com). Only the partial word you have typed is sent as a query string — no identifying information is attached. This feature can be disabled by turning off Word Predictions in Settings.

Google's use of query data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Local Storage

The following data is stored locally on your device only and is never transmitted:

  • Keyboard preferences (theme, language selections, layout choice)
  • Learned words for personalised word prediction
  • Recent emoji history

This data is stored using Android's SharedPreferences API and can be cleared at any time by clearing the app's data in your device settings.

Permissions

  • VIBRATE — for haptic feedback on keypresses. Optional; can be disabled in Settings.
  • INTERNET — for word suggestions only (see above).
  • READ_MEDIA_IMAGES / READ_EXTERNAL_STORAGE — reserved for potential future background image feature. Not currently used.
  • RECORD_AUDIO — reserved for potential future voice input feature. Not currently used.

Open Source

AksharaKB is open-source software. The full source code is available on GitHub at github.com/gihansgamage/AksharaKB. You are welcome to audit the code to verify any of the claims made in this policy.

Children's Privacy

AksharaKB is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. If you believe a child has provided information through the App, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
